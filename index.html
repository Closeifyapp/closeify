Privacy Policy
Last Updated: April 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Closeify ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Closeify sales coaching application ("Services"). By using Closeify, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Closeify is an AI-powered real-time sales coaching tool that listens to live sales calls and provides instant objection rebuttals.

1. Recording Consent — Important

You are solely responsible for obtaining all legally required consents from all parties on any call before using Closeify. Recording laws vary by jurisdiction. In many states and countries, all-party consent is required. We are not responsible for your compliance with applicable recording laws.

2. Information We Collect
Information You Provide
Product setup data (product name, advantages, competitors, pricing) to personalize rebuttals
Call outcome logs (win/loss) you manually enter
Support requests or feedback you send us directly
Automatically Collected
Voice/speech data processed in real time — not permanently stored on our servers
Rebuttal history and call analysis results generated during your session
Device info: device type, OS, browser, IP address, usage patterns
Session data (call duration, objection counts, stats) stored locally on your device
3. How We Use Your Information
Provide real-time AI-powered sales rebuttals and coaching analysis
Personalize responses based on your product setup
Train and improve our AI models
Respond to support requests
Monitor for security issues and prevent misuse
Comply with legal obligations
4. How We Share Your Information
AI Processing Providers

Rebuttal generation uses third-party AI APIs (including Anthropic). Voice transcriptions are transmitted to these services for processing. These providers have their own privacy policies.

Infrastructure Providers

We use Cloudflare and similar providers to operate our services. They process data on our behalf under data protection agreements.

We Do Not Sell Your Data

Closeify does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

5. Data Storage

Most Closeify data — call history, win/loss records, product setup, stats — is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. We do not maintain a central database of this information.

Voice data is processed in real time and is not permanently stored on our servers. We implement commercially reasonable security measures for any transmitted data.

6. Your Rights
Access: View all data stored by Closeify in your browser's localStorage
Deletion: Clear all data using the Clear buttons in-app or by clearing site data in your browser
Opt-out: Stop the listening feature at any time by tapping Stop Listening
Correction: Update your product setup and preferences at any time

California and Nevada residents have additional rights under CCPA and Nevada law. We do not sell personal information. Contact us for more information.

7. Children's Privacy

Closeify is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. Contact us if you believe we have inadvertently collected such information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be indicated by an updated date at the top. Continued use of the Services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
